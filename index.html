This page contains the images of the 3 of my favourite places in IIT Kharagpur.




The 3 places are Puri Gate , Main Building and Clock Tower.





The Iconic Entrance, Puri Gate which signifies the beginning of the campus, and the first thing when someone enters the college gets to see.





The Monument which stands in the centre of the campus is the Main Building which is the main attraction and the first thing that comes to mind when thinking about IIT Kharagpur.





The Clock Tower (Donated by the Alumni), one end of the 2.2 which rings at the stroke of every hour tells students the time and even the importance of it.
